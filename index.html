Biography

I am currently working toward the PhD degree and supervised by Prof. Desheng Zhang in the Department of Computer Science, Rutgers University.

My research interests lie in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Large Language Models (LLMs), with a strong background in real-world industrial applications. My work focuses on bridging advanced research and Scalable&Complex real-world deployment. I have extensive experience collaborating with industry partners, driving practical solutions from prototypes to production..
